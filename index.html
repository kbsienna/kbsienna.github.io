Hear Me

Hear Me is a psychologically dark erotic novel that begins in a post-apocalyptic world. Rhea survives by staying unseen and unheard, moving carefully through its ruins, where survival depends on caution and usefulness. When Kane breaks that fragile balance, her world is flipped upside down.

Taken from what remains of her life, Rhea is forced into a new reality where autonomy is stripped away and survival demands submission, adaptation, and restraint. When an opportunity finally emerges, she is faced with a grim choice to make a sacrifice in order to protect what truly matters.
